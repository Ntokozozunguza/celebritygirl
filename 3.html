ABOUT ME

With over five years in the fashion industry, I've had the privilege of working with renowned photographers and brands across the globe. My journey began in Milan and has taken me from the runways of Paris to the studios of New York.

I specialize in editorial fashion, bringing a unique blend of sophistication and contemporary edge to every project. My work has been featured in leading fashion publications and campaigns for luxury brands.

Beyond modeling, I'm passionate about mentoring emerging talent and sharing insights about the evolving landscape of fashion and beauty.
